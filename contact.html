Why Contact Photobooth Guys?

Whether you are planning a wedding, corporate event, birthday party, or Christmas celebration, our team is here to help you find the perfect photo booth experience. We provide premium photo booth hire across Ireland, from Dublin and Galway to Belfast and beyond.

What happens after you enquire?

  • We will reply within 24 hours (usually much sooner during business hours)
  • We will suggest the best booth type for your event and venue
  • You will receive a personalised quote with no hidden fees
  • We will check availability for your date and confirm your booking

Serving All of Ireland

Based in Dublin, we deliver nationwide to every county. No matter where your event is, we will be there with a fully set up booth, a professional attendant, and all the props and accessories included. We cover weddings at top venues in Meath, Kildare, Wicklow, Westmeath, Galway, Limerick, Tipperary and Northern Ireland.

Not sure which booth to choose?

That is completely fine — tell us about your event in the form above and we will recommend the best option. From open air photo booths and selfie mirrors to 360 video booths and audio guestbooks, we have something for every occasion and budget. Our most popular choices for weddings are the selfie mirror and open air booth, while corporate clients love the 360 booth and branded activations.
