360 Photo Booth: Everything You Need to Know

If you have been to any event recently, chances are you have seen clips of people striking poses on a spinning platform while a camera whirls around them, capturing cinematic slow-motion footage. That is a 360 photo booth, and it has quickly become the most in-demand entertainment feature at weddings, parties, and corporate events across Ireland.

But what exactly is a 360 photo booth? How does it work? And is it right for your event? In this guide, we cover absolutely everything you need to know about 360 photo booth hire in Ireland.

What Is a 360 Photo Booth?

A 360 photo booth is a platform-based video experience that captures guests from every angle. Unlike a traditional photo booth that takes a single static image, a 360 booth uses a camera mounted on a rotating arm that circles around the subject (or subjects) standing on an elevated platform.

The result is a stunning slow-motion video that lasts around 15–30 seconds, showing the subject from every angle. These clips are instantly shareable and look incredible on social media — which is exactly why they have exploded in popularity over the past few years.

How Does a 360 Photo Booth Work?

The process is simple and intuitive for guests:

  1. Step onto the platform — Guests step up onto a sturdy, circular platform (typically around 1 metre in diameter).
  2. Strike a pose — Our booth attendant cues the start, and guests can pose, dance, throw confetti, or simply look fabulous.
  3. The camera rotates — A high-speed camera on a stabilised arm rotates around the platform, capturing the action from every angle.
  4. AI effects are applied — The footage is automatically processed with your chosen effects — slow motion, speed ramps, custom overlays, music, and more.
  5. Instant sharing — Within seconds, guests receive their finished video via QR code directly to their phone, ready to share on Instagram, TikTok, or WhatsApp.

The entire experience takes about 60–90 seconds per group, meaning your 360 booth can entertain a large number of guests throughout the evening.

What Makes a 360 Booth Different from a Regular Photo Booth?

While traditional photo booths capture still images or GIFs, a 360 photo booth produces dynamic video content. Here are the key differences:

Video vs. photos: A 360 booth creates cinematic video clips rather than static photographs. The slow-motion effect and rotating perspective make even casual poses look dramatic and professional.

Social media appeal: 360 videos are tailor-made for platforms like Instagram Reels and TikTok. They are far more likely to be shared and engaged with than a standard photo booth image.

The experience: There is something uniquely exciting about standing on an elevated platform with a camera spinning around you. It feels like being in a music video, and guests genuinely queue up to have their turn.

Brand activations: For corporate events, the 360 booth is a marketing powerhouse. Custom-branded overlays, hashtags, and effects mean every shared video promotes your brand organically.

Space Requirements and Setup

One of the most common questions we get is about space. Here is what you need to know:

  • Minimum floor space: Approximately 3m x 3m (10ft x 10ft) of clear, flat floor space
  • Ceiling height: A minimum of 2.5m (8ft) to allow for the camera arm to rotate freely
  • Surface: A flat, level surface is essential — the platform needs to be stable and safe. Carpeted floors, wooden floors, concrete, and smooth tiles all work well
  • Power: A single standard power outlet within 5 metres of the setup location
  • Indoor or outdoor: The 360 booth works in both settings, though outdoor use requires shelter from rain and wind

If you are unsure whether your venue can accommodate a 360 booth, simply get in touch and we will advise. We have set up at hundreds of venues across Ireland, from grand hotel ballrooms to marquees and even pubs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our standard 360 platform comfortably fits 2–4 people at a time. For larger groups, we can advise on options to ensure everyone is captured in the shot.

Yes. Our 360 platform is designed with safety as a priority. It features a non-slip surface and a low step-up height. Our trained attendant is always present to assist guests on and off the platform.
