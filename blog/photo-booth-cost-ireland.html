How Much Does a Photo Booth Cost in Ireland? 2026 Pricing Guide

Planning an event and wondering how much a photo booth costs in Ireland? You are not alone. Photo booth hire is one of the most popular entertainment choices for weddings, corporate events, and parties across the country, but pricing can vary widely depending on the type of booth, your location, and the package you choose.

In this comprehensive 2026 guide, we break down the cost of every type of photo booth available for hire in Ireland, explain exactly what is included in the price, and share tips for getting the best value for your budget.

Photo Booth Hire Prices by Booth Type

The single biggest factor in photo booth hire price is the type of booth you choose. Each offers a different experience, and prices reflect the technology, setup, and overall wow factor involved. Here is what you can expect to pay in Ireland in 2026:

Open Air Photo Booth — from €680

The open air photo booth is one of our most popular options and a fantastic all-rounder. With no walls or enclosure, it accommodates large groups easily and produces high-quality prints on the spot. It is ideal for weddings, parties, and corporate events where you want maximum guest involvement.

Selfie Mirror — from €730

The selfie mirror is a full-length interactive mirror that guides guests through the photo-taking process with on-screen animations and prompts. It is a real showstopper at weddings and black-tie events, offering a premium feel that guests absolutely love.

360 Photo Booth — from €750

The 360 photo booth captures stunning slow-motion video as a camera arm rotates around guests standing on a platform. These clips are perfect for social media and have become hugely popular at corporate brand activations, parties, and weddings. Read our full 360 photo booth guide for more details.

Glam Photo Booth — from €730

The glam photo booth delivers a Kardashian-style black-and-white photography experience with professional studio lighting. It produces striking, fashion-forward portraits that look like they belong in a magazine. A favourite for hen parties, milestone birthdays, and glamorous events.

Vintage Tripod Photo Booth — from €680

The vintage tripod photo booth combines a retro aesthetic with modern technology. It is compact, stylish, and fits perfectly into rustic or boho-themed weddings and intimate gatherings.

Magazine Photo Booth — from €1,200

The magazine photo booth is our premium offering, creating personalised magazine-style covers featuring your guests. It is the ultimate choice for brand activations, fashion events, and corporate launches where you want to make a lasting impression.

AI Photo Booth — from €680

The AI photo booth uses artificial intelligence to transform guest photos into creative, themed artwork in real time. From cartoon styles to fantasy settings, it delivers a completely unique experience that guests will not stop talking about.

Enclosed Photo Booth — from €680

The enclosed photo booth is the classic curtained booth experience. It offers privacy, nostalgia, and a cosy space for guests to let loose with props and printouts. Perfect for weddings and parties where you want that traditional photo strip feel.

Audio Phone Guestbook — from €350

The audio phone guestbook is a retro-style telephone that records voice messages from your guests. It is a beautiful, heartfelt alternative to a traditional guestbook and pairs wonderfully with any photo booth for a complete guest entertainment package.

Guinness Pint Printer — from €680

The Guinness pint printer prints custom images directly onto the foam of a freshly poured pint of Guinness. It is a uniquely Irish experience that is perfect for corporate events, brand activations, and festival bars.

Factors That Affect Photo Booth Hire Pricing

While the starting prices above give you a solid benchmark, several factors can influence the final cost of your photo booth hire in Ireland:

Duration: Most packages are based on a standard hire period (typically 3 hours). Extending the hire will increase the cost, but additional hours are often available at a reduced rate.

Travel distance: We are based in Athlone, Westmeath, right in the heart of Ireland. Delivery is included within a generous radius, but events in more remote locations may incur a small travel supplement.

Customisation: Bespoke branding, custom backdrops, or themed setups beyond the standard package may carry an additional cost, particularly for corporate events with specific brand guidelines.

Peak season: Saturdays in June, July, August, and December are our busiest periods. Booking during off-peak times can sometimes offer better availability and value.

Multiple booths: Hiring more than one booth for the same event? We offer bundle discounts that can save you a significant amount compared to booking separately.

Tips for Getting the Best Value

Here are our top tips for making your photo booth budget stretch further:

Book early: We offer early booking discounts of 10–15% for events booked well in advance. The sooner you confirm, the more you save.

Bundle your booths: If you want more than one experience at your event (for example, a 360 booth and an audio guestbook), bundling them together gives you a better price than hiring each individually.

Choose off-peak dates: Friday evenings, Sundays, and midweek events often come with more flexibility on pricing. If your date is flexible, it is worth asking what deals are available.

Get a quote: Every event is different, and we tailor our packages to suit your needs. The best way to get an accurate price is to get in touch and tell us about your event.
