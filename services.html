Our Services

Photo Booth Hire Services Ireland

We run 13 different booth types across Ireland — everything from our best-selling selfie mirror to the 360 video platform and a Guinness pint printer that prints your face on a pint. Every hire includes delivery, setup, an attendant, and packdown.

We started with a single photo booth in Athlone back in 2018 and now cover events nationwide — from Dublin weddings and Galway festivals to corporate launches in Cork and Belfast. Below you will find every booth and service we offer. Each one can be customised with branded overlays, custom backdrops, and bespoke print layouts to match your event.

Our most popular option and the one we bring to the most weddings. Uses a Canon DSLR with studio lighting, so the print quality is noticeably sharper than tablet-based booths. Guests can print, create boomerangs, or share digitally via QR code.

  • Unlimited prints included
  • Boomerang & GIF mode
  • QR code digital sharing
