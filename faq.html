Booth Types

Understanding Our Photo Booths

A 360 photo booth is a platform guests stand on while a camera arm rotates around them, capturing stunning slow-motion video from every angle. The result is a cinematic 360-degree clip with custom overlays and AI effects that guests can share instantly via QR code. It is our most popular booth for corporate events and parties.

A selfie mirror (also called a magic mirror) is a full-length interactive mirror with a hidden camera and touchscreen. Guests interact with on-screen animations, strike a pose, and receive instant dye-sublimated prints. It also offers digital sharing via QR codes, boomerangs, and GIFs. It is perfect for weddings and elegant events.

An open air photo booth has no walls, allowing larger groups and giving a more social, visible experience. An enclosed photo booth offers a private, curtained space for more intimate photos. Open air is better for large groups and corporate events; enclosed suits guests who want a bit more privacy. Both produce professional DSLR-quality photos.

An AI photo booth uses artificial intelligence to enhance photos with creative effects like face swap, style transfer, themed filters, and background replacement. Guests get unique, shareable content that goes far beyond a standard photo. It is a brilliant talking point at parties and brand activations.

Our audio phone guestbook is a vintage-style telephone. Guests pick up the handset and leave a voice message, which is recorded as an MP3 file. After the event, you receive all messages to download and keep forever -- a beautiful, personal alternative to a traditional written guestbook. Particularly popular at weddings.
