Photo Booth Hire Meath FAQ

Meath photo booth hire starts from €680 for our vintage tripod booth. Selfie mirrors start from €730, 360 booths from €750, and premium magazine booths from €1,200. The final price depends on your chosen booth, hire duration, and any bespoke additions. Get a free Meath quote.

As we are based in the Midlands, Meath is well within our regular coverage zone. Travel fees are minimal for most Meath venues and are often waived entirely. We confirm any applicable charge upfront so you know exactly what to expect.

We have extensive experience at Tankardstown House, Ballymagarvey Village, Knightsbrook Hotel in Trim, Dunboyne Castle, Bellinter House, and Trim Castle Hotel. We know the layout, power access, and best placement spots at each of these venues.

Meath is one of Ireland's top wedding destinations, so we recommend booking 6-10 weeks in advance. Saturday dates during peak wedding season (May to September) are particularly popular. Booking early may also qualify you for an early-bird discount.

Most booths require approximately 2m x 2m of floor space and a standard 3-pin socket within 5 metres. We bring all extension leads, lighting, and equipment ourselves.
